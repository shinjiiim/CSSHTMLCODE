OFF-SHOP

Designing a User-Centric Online Shopping App from Scratch Using Design Thinking Methodology

Role UX/UI Designer, Researcher
Deliverables 150+ pages, Light & Dark Mode
Tools Figma, User Research, Prototyping

Context

This project is an online shopping app designed from scratch to offer a simple and user-friendly experience. With new features added, the design thinking process was structured to prioritize users' real needs, aiming to create a successful, user-centric product.

As my first successful independent UI/UX design project, OFF-SHOP represents a comprehensive application of design thinking methodology, resulting in over 150 pages with both light and dark modes.

Problem Statement

In this project, I applied the design thinking approach. The first step involved empathizing with users to uncover their needs. During this phase, the primary goal was to identify the users' needs, motivations, and challenges.

üîª Existing Pain Points

  • Limited product customization options
  • Difficulty accessing rare or unavailable products
  • Lack of engaging features like purchase sharing
  • Generic shopping experiences that don't cater to individual preferences

Design Process: Discovery Phase

Following the design thinking methodology, I conducted comprehensive user research through both qualitative and quantitative methods to gain deep insights into users' online shopping experiences.

üìä Quantitative Research

Conducted surveys with users to understand shopping frequency, preferences, and feature importance on a scale of 1-5

üí¨ Qualitative Interviews

In-depth interviews to explore shopping behaviors, feature preferences, and unmet needs in existing apps

üë• User Personas

Created detailed personas representing users with different needs, continuously referenced throughout the design process

üîç Competitor Analysis

Evaluated Iranian apps in the same field to identify strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities for differentiation

Sample Research Questions

Quantitative Questions

  • How many times a month do you shop online?
  • How many times a month do you browse online shops without purchasing?
  • Rate the importance of requesting unavailable products (1-5)
  • Rate your interest in creating personalized product packs (1-5)
  • Rate the importance of discovering good stores (1-5)
  • Rate your interest in sharing purchase photos (1-5)

Qualitative Questions

  • Do you shop online? How do you usually do it?
  • What features in an online shopping app appeal to you?
  • Have you wanted to request a product you couldn't find?
  • How important is knowing which store you're buying from?
  • Do you like buying in bundles? What type of bundles?
  • Have you taken photos of purchases? Would you share them?

üîç Key Research Insights

Users sought ease of purchase, product customization, and access to special offers. They highly value personalization and want easier access to exclusive or hard-to-find products.

Define Phase

This phase was taken to accurately define user needs and problems:

Key Questions Addressed:

  • Who are our users?
  • What role does online shopping play in their lifestyle?
  • What are their needs and expectations?
  • How do they currently make purchases?
  • Which of their purchases are made online?
  • What are their expectations from an online store?

Competitor Analysis

I conducted a comprehensive competitor analysis evaluating how Iranian apps in the same field are performing. This involved creating a detailed table outlining the strengths and weaknesses of competing apps to identify opportunities for differentiation.

Develop Phase: Ideation

At this stage, I generated various ideas to address the identified problems, resulting in innovative features that would set OFF-SHOP apart:

üéÅ Custom Pack Creation

Users can create personalized product bundles tailored to their preferences, allowing for unique shopping experiences and better value

üìù Product Request Feature

Users can request products they can't find, helping them access rare or typically unavailable items while providing valuable data to retailers
